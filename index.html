VISIT OUR CHURCH

We are a Church that believes in Jesus, a church that loves God and People. We have an active unique community where children and teenagers learn and grow to become Christ Ambassadors to showcase the Kingdom of God to the world.

INTRODUCTION

At Word Of Life Assembly Junior Church, we believe that every one has a unique purpose and role in our faith community. Our vision is to empower young individuals to discover their God-given gifts, cultivate their faith, and actively contribute to the church and society. We offer a variety of engaging programs tailored specifically for teenagers and children.

OUR DUTIES AND OUR RESPONSIBILITIES:

Serving in the worship team or choir Assisting in children's ministries Volunteering in community outreach projects Participating in youth leadership programs Supporting fundraising initiatives for charitable causes.Study and Apply the Bible Be Christ Ambassadors: Represent Christ Sharing Our Faith: Preaching the Gospel Using Social Media and Technology for Good Praying for Others

OUR VISIONS:

Developing a personal and authentic relationship with God, fostering a deep and intimate connection through prayer, worship, and Bible study. Aspiring to grow as disciples of Christ and making a meaningful impact in the world by actively engaging in acts of service and positively influencing our schools and communities. We envision being equipped as leaders empowered to inspire our peers and future generations, leaving a lasting legacy of faithfulness and commitment to Christ.

OUR MISSIONS:

We seek to understand and live out our identity as children of God, displaying His love, grace, and character in our lives. Growing in our faith, Sharing the Gospel, Serving and ministering; We strive to serve others within the church and the broader community, using our gifts and talents to meet needs, bring comfort, and extend compassion to those around us. We lead by example through our words, behavior, and lifestyle, inspiring others to follow Christ.

OUR VALUES:

As Children and Teenagers Faith, love, compassion, forgiveness, respect, humility, and responsibility are our core values. We bring fresh perspectives, vibrant energy, and a willingness to explore and deepen their faith, inspiring and challenging others. Our genuine curiosity and openness create opportunities for growth, fostering a culture of learning and discipleship.
